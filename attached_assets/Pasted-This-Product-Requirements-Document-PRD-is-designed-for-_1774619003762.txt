This **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** is designed for a lightweight, high-impact web game titled **"Daphne’s Root"**. It is optimized for an AI agent to build using a single-file structure (HTML/CSS/JS) or a simple React/Vite scaffold on Replit.

-----

# PRD: Daphne’s Root (Metamorphosis Clicker)

## 1\. Project Overview

A narrative-driven incremental game (clicker) where the player takes the role of the nymph Daphne at the moment of her transformation. The goal is to "solidify" your form into a Laurel tree to escape Apollo’s pursuit, using original Latin lines as both narrative milestones and mechanical upgrades.

## 2\. Core Loop

1.  **Generate "Numen" (Divine Essence):** Click the central sprite (Daphne/Tree).
2.  **Purchase Metamorphosis Upgrades:** Use Numen to unlock lines of Latin text.
3.  **Resist the Sun:** Apollo’s "Heat" bar increases over time; upgrades keep the bark cool and the roots deep.
4.  **Completion:** Once all 20 lines (focused on your 545–559 selection) are unlocked, the transformation is complete, and the "Triumph" ending triggers.

-----

## 3\. Visual & UI Requirements

  * **The Sprite:** A central canvas element. It starts as a silhouette of a woman and gradually evolves (adding bark textures, leaves, and roots) as upgrades are purchased.
  * **The Scroll:** A side panel where the 20 Latin lines appear one by one.
  * **The "Oscula" Warning:** A visual pulsing red glow when Apollo's "hand" (the Heat bar) gets too close, based on line 554 (*sentit adhuc trepidare*).

-----

## 4\. Feature Roadmap & Latin Integration

The game is divided into **four "Acts"** based on your selected text.

### Act I: The Prayer (*Prece Finita*)

*Focus: Establishing the transformation.*

  * **Line 545:** *“fer, pater,” inquit “opem\!”* \* **Unlock:** Manual clicks generate +2 Numen.
  * **Line 548:** *vix prece finitā torpor gravis occupat artūs.*
      * **Unlock:** "Torpor" Auto-Clicker. Generates 1 Numen/sec.

### Act II: The Hardening (*Sub Cortice*)

*Focus: Defensive upgrades against Apollo’s persistence.*

  * **Line 549:** *mollia cinguntur tenui praecordia libro.*
      * **Upgrade:** **Tenui Libro (Thin Bark).** Reduces the rate at which Apollo’s "Heat" increases.
  * **Line 551:** *pes modo tam velox pigris radicibus haeret.*
      * **Upgrade:** **Pigris Radicibus (Sluggish Roots).** Multiplies Numen generation by 2x.

### Act III: The Rejection (*Refugit Oscula*)

*Focus: Reaching the "Tree" state.*

  * **Line 553:** *Hanc quoque Phoebus amat...*
      * **Event:** Apollo’s hand appears on the UI.
  * **Line 556:** *oscula dat ligno; refugit tamen oscula lignum.*
      * **Upgrade:** **Refugit Oscula.** A "recoil" mechanic that pushes Apollo’s Heat bar back by 20% whenever a line is completed.

### Act IV: The Eternal Laurel (*Arbor Eris*)

*Focus: The final legacy.*

  * **Line 558:** *arbor eris certe dixit “mea\! semper habebunt...”*
      * **Upgrade:** **Semper Habebunt.** Final multiplier.
  * **Line 559:** *tu ducibus Latiis aderis, cum laeta Triumphum...*
      * **Endgame:** The screen fades to gold; the tree is fully formed. The "Triumph" text displays.

## 5\. Technical Specifications (for the Agent)

### Data Structure

```javascript
const storyNodes = [
  { line: "fer, pater, inquit opem!", trigger: 10, effect: "clickPower" },
  { line: "vix prece finitā torpor gravis occupat artūs", trigger: 100, effect: "autoClick" },
  // ... etc
];
```

### Game Logic

  * **Resource:** `numen` (Integer).
  * **Apollo's Heat:** A float (0 to 100). If it hits 100, the "heart trembles" (line 554) and Numen generation slows by 50% until the player clicks "Refuse" (Refugit).
  * **State Management:** The agent should use `localStorage` so the user can refresh their Replit browser without losing their metamorphosis progress.
